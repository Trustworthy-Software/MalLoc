The RuMMs malware sample used in our evaluation is a real-world Android malware. To maintain research ethics and comply with data sharing policies, we do not distribute the APK directly. Instead, please request access from the official MalRadar repository:

https://malradar.github.io

1. Request access to the MalRadar repository
